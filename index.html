Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 mintues

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese,diced vegetables, cooked meats, herb

Intrustions

  1. 1.Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. 2.Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over meadium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. 3.Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbing, pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. 4.Add filling(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the omelette.
  5. 5.Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  6. 6.Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
